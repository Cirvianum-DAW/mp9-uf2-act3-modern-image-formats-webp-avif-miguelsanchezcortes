What is the most optimal format for this type of image and why?

the optimal format for an image depends on its content and intended use. In general, for photographs, use JPEG; for images with transparency or sharp edges, use PNG; for simple graphics or vectors, use SVG; and for animations, use GIF or APNG.

What happens when we save it in JPEG format?

Saving an image in JPEG format involves lossy compression, reducing file size by discarding some image data. This process can lead to visible artifacts, especially in areas with sharp edges or high contrast.

If our website has a white background, what format would be the most optimal and why?

PNG is the best format for images on a website with a white background because it supports transparency and maintains high quality without compression loss.
